SRCCON 2015 Lightning Talks

SRCCON 2015 will continue its tradition of fun evening events with hosting Lightning Talks Thursday night. Lightning talks are a set of presentations each of which is a strict 5 minutes (seriously, only 5 minutes). The topic is open to anything you want — we want to hear about your surprising interests, hobbies, and passions (journalism related or not).
