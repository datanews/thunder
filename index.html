SRCCON 2015 Lightning Talks

SRCCON 2015 will continue its tradition of fun evening events with lightning talks Thursday night. A lightning talk is a strict 5 minutes (or less) presentation. The topic is open to anything you want — we want to hear about your surprising interests, hobbies, and passions (journalism related or not).
